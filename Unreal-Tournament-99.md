* ut-server-436.tar (Latest Official Version)
* UTPGPatch451.tar.bz2 (Unofficial Patch http://www.utpg.org)
* UTBonusPack.zip
* UTBonusPack2.zip (maps only)
* UTInoxxPack.zip
* UTBonusPack4.zip
